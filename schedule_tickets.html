When & How

The Santa Special operates on the three weekends running up Christmas. It departs up to three times a day from Connolly Station on Amiens Street in Dublin, travelling to Maynooth on a return journey. Trip times are around two and a half hours, but they can vary depending on how the engine’s mood 😀.


Tickets go on sale 1st of June and can be purchased on Eventbrite, costing €18.00 each. Ticket price includes a selection box gift for kids and a tea/coffee with a mince pie for big kids (or yes can have a selection box instead).
