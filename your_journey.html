Full Steam Ahead

Departing from Connolly Station, much of our route follows the course of the Royal Canal, passing many landmarks as we do. We’ll huff and puff our way through the north side of Dublin City, taking in Drumcondra and Castleknock, before emerging into the Irish countryside past Coolmine. From there it’s onto Lucan and Leixlip, before we shunt up in Maynooth for a quick break (time for photos with Santa and his Elves) before turning around again. All the while you get to sit back and have fun with the kids, enjoy the live music, and indulge in the refreshments we have on offer.
