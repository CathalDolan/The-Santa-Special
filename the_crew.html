Santa's Little Helpers

Just like his factories in the North Pole, Santa needs a whole team of volunteers to run his special train. In fact he needs nearly fifty of them on every journey! Everyone from sandwich makers, to fitters and engineers are involved in making sure your journey on the Santa Special is just that, special!

Santa & His Elves

Our most important crew members are of course Santa and his elves. Technically they run the entire operation, but because their time is taken up making sure all of the children onboard are happy, they let others handle the messy work.

The Engineers

These are the folks that shovel the coal to keep the fires burning, who open the valves to keep the steam pumping, grease the cogs to keep the wheels turning, and drive the train to keep us all smiling! Their soot covered faces make them easy to spot.

The Bar & Kitchen

When it comes to refreshments, we have you covered. Apart from teas, coffees, minerals and a full bar, we also serve the best mince pies in the country, while our sandwiches fillings are the stuff of legend!

The Safety Officers

The Management

When the big boss, the man in red, is off on his rounds, he hands the reins over to the Train Manager. Their job is to make sure that everything runs without a hitch.

The Musicians

Expect to see the musicians before you hear them as they wander from carriage to carriage, singing, dancing, joking, and generally getting our guests in the mood for the main man. Impromptu platform or bar-side sing-alongs are to be expected.
