A Most Magical Journey

Get your Christmas jumpers on, the kids packed up and prepare for the most magical of Christmas experiences. It’s time to board the Santa Special! Join Santa Claus, his elves and a host of others as you and the kids steam your way into the festive season.

Meet Santa & His Elves

It wouldn’t be Christmas without the big man in red and his helpful elves. You’ll meet them all of them onboard, where there’ll be gifts and “Ho! Ho! Ho!’s” a plenty.

Take a Trip Back in Time

Step onboard the Santa Special and journey back to a different era. Our engines and carriages have all past their 70th birthdays, and some are nearly a century old!

Sit Back & Enjoy

Once settled into your vintage carriage, sit back and enjoy the trip. Sing along with our musicians, savour a drink from the bar, or just take in the beautiful city and countryside.
